The garden house

This house design seamlessly blends indoor and outdoor spaces, creating a harmonious unity between the home and nature. The use of large windows and sliding doors allow natural light to flood the interior, and blur the lines between inside and outside. The incorporation of materials such as wood and stone, and the incorporation of lush greenery, create a sense of being at one with the surroundings. The outdoor living spaces, such as a patio or terrace, offer the perfect setting for entertaining or simply enjoying the beauty of nature. This home design truly brings the outside in, creating a sanctuary that harmoniously blends with its surroundings.

The art house

This house design is inspired by the works of the renowned architect Antoni Gaudi. It features the use of organic, flowing forms and vibrant colors, reminiscent of Gaudi's signature style. The use of curved lines and undulating shapes create a sense of movement and fluidity. The incorporation of natural elements, such as stone and greenery, add to the overall organic feel. The play of light and shadow, through the use of stained glass windows, creates a dynamic and vibrant atmosphere. This home design, in the style of Gaudi, is not only visually stunning, but also an architectural masterpiece that will stand the test of time.
